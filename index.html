At CHS, we believe every haircut tells a story. Your story. That's why we don't just cut hair — we craft experiences that reflect who you are and where you're going. Each chair is our stage, each client our canvas, each cut our masterpiece.

Tony brings over a decade of precision barbering to every appointment. But it's not just about technical skill — it's about understanding people. Knowing when to talk, when to listen, and when to let the craft speak for itself.
