Our Story

We Listen First. Then We Create.

CUT is a modern men's grooming studio built on a single idea — that a great haircut begins with a real conversation. About who you are, how you carry yourself, and how you want the world to see you.

Located in the heart of Philadelphia on South 13th Street, our studio is a welcoming space for every man. From the moment you step in — greeted with fresh coffee, a friendly face, and zero judgment — to the moment you walk out with a cut that earns compliments, the experience is entirely yours.

Every service includes a complimentary shampoo and conditioning treatment. Because the details always matter.
